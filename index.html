About Us

Welcome to "Who Will Pay?", the ultimate tool for making fair and fun decisions! Whether you're trying to decide who gets the next round of drinks, who has to do the dishes, or any other group choice, our spinner wheel is here to help.

Our mission is simple: to eliminate arguments and indecision with a single spin. Just add the names of the participants, hit the spin button, and let fate decide. It's fast, easy, and always impartial.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The names you enter into the spinner wheel are stored only in your browser's local session and are not sent to our servers. Once you close the browser tab, this information is gone.

Cookies

We use local storage to save your theme preference (dark or light mode). This is a simple key-value pair and does not track you across other websites.
